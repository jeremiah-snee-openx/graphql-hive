# Introduction

GraphQL Hive is a schema registry for GraphQL where you can host, manage and collaborate on all your GraphQL schemas and operations irrespective of the strategy you are using underneath, be it schema stitching, federation, or just a good old monolith.

GraphQL Hive is currently available as a hosted service to be used by all and we take care of the heavy lifting behind the scenes be it managing the registry, scaling it as per your needs, etc. leaving your time for just the most important things at hand.
